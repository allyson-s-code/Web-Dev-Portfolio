About Me.

Hi! I'm Allyson and I am a front-end developer and maker based in Brooklyn, NY.

I come to coding with a background in fine art and home product design. I have always been interested in drawing, painting, and the physical side of making. It wasn’t until recently that I discovered the satisfaction of building things for the web. I love figuring out how to code responsive websites and user interfaces. Most of all I love learning new things every day and putting things out into the world that help make the web a little brighter.

When I’m not coding I’m painting, exploring nature, baking deliciousness, or learning ALL about Minecraft from my 6-year-old.

I can’t wait to work with you!
